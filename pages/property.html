Villa Celeste Luxury Estate

Suspended between the sea and the sky, Villa Celeste is a masterpiece of Mediterranean architecture. Located in the most exclusive part of Positano, this historic estate has been meticulously restored to offer an unparalleled luxury experience. Every corner of the villa is designed to maximise the breathtaking panoramas of the Amalfi Coast, featuring expansive terraces, lush lemon groves, and a spectacular infinity pool that seems to spill into the cobalt horizon.

Your 3-Day Amalfi Dream

1

Arrival & Coastal Sunset

Private transfer from Naples, welcome cocktail on the terrace, and a curated four-course dinner by your private chef.

2

Private Boat to Capri

Charter a classic Riva boat for a day tour of Capri and the Faraglioni rocks, with swim stops at secret coves only accessible by sea.

3

Path of the Gods Hike

A guided trek along the world-renowned Sentiero degli Dei, followed by a wine tasting at a local cliffside vineyard in Praiano.
